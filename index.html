About Us


SK-Flower was founded in India by renowned designer florist Sathish Kumar, one of the India leading floral design specialists, and provides exquisite flowers for all events and occasions. We have an impressive store in India!

At SK-Flower we pride ourselves on delivering the very best flowers, backed up by a friendly and professional service. SK-Flower specialises in:

Wedding Flowers: We cater for weddings of all sizes, including Asian weddings, providing bridal bouquets to buttonholes, table centres to venue decorations and prop hire.
Event Flowers: We’ve decorated more venues for more companies than it’s possible to mention here with companies returning to us year-after-year. Whatever your event and budget, SK-Flower can help.
Corporate Flowers: SK-Flower can deliver and maintain floral displays and plants for offices, restaurants, hotels and retailers, providing the professional finishing touch.
Floral Tributes: We understand how important floral funeral tributes are, which is why we’ll deal with any requests with professionalism and dignity, creating a fitting personal design.

As well as providing flowers for all occasions and events, the SK-Flower Design School is a highly-acclaimed training provider, specialising in teaching the latest floral design skills. From beginner through to experienced professional looking to develop specialist skills, the Flower Power Design School offers a series of themed courses, with topics covered including Asian wedding design, event floristry and much more.
